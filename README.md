## What is JobCraft AI?

JobCraft AI is a suite of free AI-powered tools built for modern job seekers. Upload your resume, paste a job description, and get a tailored, ATS-optimized resume delivered to your inbox in under 2 minutes.

No sign-up. No credit card. Just results.

## How It Works

1. **Upload your resume** — paste or upload in any format
2. **Add the job description** — URL or plain text
3. **Get your optimized resume** — delivered to your inbox, ATS-ready

---

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend** — Pure HTML/CSS/JS (GitHub Pages)
- **Automation** — n8n workflow
- **AI** — Google Gemini API
- **PDF Generation** — PDFBolt
- **Email Delivery** — Gmail SMTP
- **Domain** — Namecheap (`jobcraftai.me`)
